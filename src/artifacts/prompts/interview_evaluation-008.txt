[00:23] Maria (Candidate): Most companies structure product orgs by function — one PM per feature area or engineering team. That works fine tactically. Where it breaks down is strategic coherence. At Prism, I restructured from functional alignment to customer journey alignment. PMs owned segments of the customer lifecycle — acquisition, onboarding, expansion — rather than engineering teams.
